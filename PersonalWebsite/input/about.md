## Creative

I'm a software developer and 3D modeller.

I mostly work with Unity and C#, and blender. I've also worked with Java in the past and enjoyed it, though that's been quite a while ago.

These days I spend most of my free time working on Unity projects, mostly focused on VR content and experiences. 

Simultaneously I am a Maintainer for [Prism Launcher](https://prismlauncher.org). As that project is C++ based, I mostly spend my time on meta-discussion, long-term plans and general project management. 
From time to time I try to contribute code to the launcher itself, but that's mostly limited to few smaller changes, as I'm not a C++ expert.

## Hobbies

When I am not working on my Projects, I play mostly PvE story games like the Horizon series (Zero Dawn, Forbidden West), or more sandboxy/survival games.
I'm trying to catch up with a lot of older (story) games that I missed out in the past. e.g. Halo (most parts), Dishonored, etc.
When I'm feeling bored, I sometimes play some PvP stuff like The Finals and recently ARC Raiders as well. Before it died out, Splitgate was also pretty cool.

Then there's VR. I don't really play too many games, but mostly use Social VR. Not gonna advertise my account here, but if you want to check out some platforms, you may stumble into me at some point.
I do try to get some workouts in every once in a while in Beat Saber and Audica. While audica is pretty much dead, It's still fun and quite the exercise.
